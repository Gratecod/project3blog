The Khan Academy Coding Experience

I have been on this coding journey with Khan Academy since March 15, 2022. Since then I believe that I have made very good progress with my coding efforts. One of the things that I enjoy about the course is that the instructor takes a no nonsense approach that gets you up and running immediately.

Her instructions are clear, simple, and easy to follow, and one of the big plus to doing this course are the hands on projects found at the end of each "module segments." They test your ability by building up on what you have learned, and help to bring out the creativity in you. Kudos to Khan Academy and instructor Pamela!

One of the most exciting things I've learned is how to make internal links on pages that have very long content. This is something that I have always wanted to do and often wondered how it works. Not anymore, because this course has made it so easy to do!
